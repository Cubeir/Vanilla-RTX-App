What is RTX Reactor?
It is a closed-source premium PBR texture generation too that allowed anyone to use their favorite Bedrock Edition texture packs with ray tracing.
Introduced in early 2023 as an exclusive reward for anyone who decided to support me, with a promise to eventually become free and open-source.
While there'll be major rewrites, the vision stays the same:
A software that generate packs that would be usually better or no different than when the average resource pack author tries to add ray tracing support to their texture packs (which in reality most never do, there are very few original non-vanilla texture packs for ray tracing floating around the internet)
RTX Reactor will eliminate lots of manual human effort, so that players are no longer limited to Vanilla RTX or a few Minecraft RTX capable resource packs, but instead thousands.
(This time with proper Vibrant Visuals support, and possibly under a new name)
